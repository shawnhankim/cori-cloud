Bring Your Own License (BYOL)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- BYOL is process you can use to deploy software that you already have license. 
- When you BYOL, you are responsible for managing your own licenses. 
- You are responsible for managing true ups and renewals as required 
  under your Volume Licensing agreement. 
- In addition, you must submit a new verification form when you renew your 
  agreement and when you deploy any previously unverified products.
